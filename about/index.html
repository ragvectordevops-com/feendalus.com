Our product approach

We start with the real workflow: the documents, decisions, roles, approvals, exceptions and systems that define a professional domain. AI is then applied where it can make that workflow easier to search, understand, operate or improve.

Domain-first

Each product uses the language, structures and operating patterns of its market rather than forcing unrelated teams into one generic interface.

Grounded intelligence

Retrieval, structured context and source references keep generated output connected to authorised information.

Controlled automation

Permissions, validation, audit history and human approval remain part of consequential workflows.

Expandable architecture

Modular interfaces, APIs, webhooks and reusable product patterns allow the portfolio to grow without weakening clarity.

Current portfolio

The current portfolio includes Feendalus Legal Workspace, Feendalus WooCommerce AI and Feendalus Shopify Intelligence.
