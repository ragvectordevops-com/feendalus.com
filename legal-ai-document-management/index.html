Frequently asked

Built to be configured around your firm.

A professional demo can be tailored to your practice areas, workflows, data model, permissions and integration priorities.

What can the legal AI analyse?

It can be configured to analyse authorised matter files, contracts, correspondence, transcripts, pleadings and firm knowledge while returning traceable source references for review.

Does the AI replace legal judgment?

No. The workspace is designed to assist legal professionals. Drafts, extracted facts, research and workflow actions remain subject to human review and approval.

Can access be restricted by matter or team?

Yes. The proposed platform supports role-based permissions, matter-level access, ethical walls, sharing controls and audit trails.

Can it connect to existing legal systems?

The architecture is integration-ready for productivity suites, email, cloud storage, e-signature, accounting, identity providers and approved legal data sources through APIs.

Can the solution be hosted for specific requirements?

Deployment, residency, identity, integration and security controls can be designed around the organisation’s technical and regulatory requirements.

How is a demo prepared?

We map your highest-value workflow, configure representative matters and documents, and demonstrate the user experience, AI controls, security model and integration approach.
