Privacy policy

How website information is handled.

This policy describes information handling for the public Feendalus website and email enquiries. Product deployments may require separate, customer-specific privacy terms.

Information we receive

When you email Feendalus, we receive the information you choose to provide, such as your name, organisation, email address, project requirements and message contents.

The hosting provider may also process standard technical logs, including IP address, browser type, requested page, date and time, referring page and security events. These logs support delivery, reliability and abuse prevention.

How information is used

  • To respond to enquiries and prepare requested product discussions or demonstrations
  • To operate, protect and improve the website
  • To investigate faults, misuse or security incidents
  • To meet applicable legal obligations

Cookies and analytics

The launch package does not install advertising cookies or third-party analytics. If analytics, forms or other tracking technologies are introduced, this policy and any required consent controls should be updated before activation.

Sharing and service providers

Information may be processed by service providers that support hosting, email, security or business operations. Information may also be disclosed when required by law or necessary to protect rights, systems or users. Feendalus does not sell personal information to advertisers.

Retention and security

Information is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary for the relevant enquiry, business record, security purpose or legal requirement. Reasonable administrative and technical safeguards are used, but no internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed completely secure.

Your choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights concerning access, correction, deletion, restriction or objection. Requests can be sent to info@feendalus.com. Identity verification may be required before completing a request.

Children

The public website is intended for business and professional audiences and is not directed to children.

Changes

This policy may be updated when the website, services or legal requirements change. The latest version will be posted on this page with its revision date.

Contact

Privacy questions can be sent to info@feendalus.com.
